About Us

Welcome to Mohamed Biruk and Friends' Service Works Partners Agency! Established in the 2013 Ethiopian Calendar, our agency is dedicated to forging partnerships that enhance organizational efficiency and unlock individual potential. With a focus on job creation, training, and sustainable growth, we strive to expand social services that promote inclusivity and support service providers in their quest for universal development. Join us in making a meaningful impact in our communities!

At our agency, we understand that the key to a prosperous future is a skilled and adaptable workforce. That's why we invest heavily in comprehensive training and development programs, ensuring individuals are equipped with the knowledge, skills, and resilience needed to navigate challenges and seize opportunities in a rapidly changing marketplace.

Join us on a transformative journey to empower individuals and organizations. Together, we build a brighter future through collaboration, opportunity, and shared success.

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Mohamed Biruk and Friend's Service Works Partner's Agency provide?

We offer a variety of social services, including capacity building for individuals and organizations, skill-based employee selection, digital service modernization, and solutions to improve employee standards of living and workplace efficiency.

How do you ensure mutual benefits for both employees and companies?

Our approach is to focus on increasing both individual and organizational abilities by offering personalized training, capacity-building programs, and technology-driven systems that enhance job performance and satisfaction.

What is the company's mission?

Our mission is to expand social services that are inclusive and focused on development, empowering service providers to contribute to universal progress. We also aim to introduce new ways of learning and skill-building for employees to make them more productive and capable.

What is your company's vision?

Our vision is to be recognized as a leading and highly intelligent service provider in the next 10 years. We aim to provide modern, accessible, and efficient services that solve problems, improve job efficiency, and promote technological advancement in companies.
